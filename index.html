Arab AI Predictive Security System (AAPS)

AI-Driven Preventive Security for Smart, Safe & Sustainable Cities

About the Project

AAPS is an innovative Arab research initiative designed to transform traditional reactive security into proactive, AI-driven preventive protection for smart and sustainable cities.

Starting from Palestine as a national pilot, the project integrates artificial intelligence, predictive analytics, anomaly detection, and digital threat intelligence to anticipate cyber threats, digital fraud, and urban security risks before they occur.

AAPS contributes to building secure digital ecosystems, strengthening public trust, and supporting sustainable development goals across the Arab region and globally.

WSIS & SDGs Alignment

AAPS directly supports WSIS Action Line C5 (Building confidence and security in the use of ICTs) and contributes to SDG 9, SDG 11, and SDG 16 by promoting resilient digital infrastructure, safe cities, and strong institutions.
